About us

SMILE club

SMILE, stands for Ancient Science and Technology for Modern India Life and Enlightenment ,as the name suggests smile club is an initiative of students who themselves are culturally aware and wish to spread the joy and richness of indian culture through engaging programs by which one learns the importance and significance of the indian cultures. We conduct a bagful of programs on indian culture so that the life of a student whose bags are just full of books and minds devoid of any joy experiences the depth of indian culture and the joy in following it. And we experience the joy in both following and sharing it amongst all. Thus through the multi faced activities we try to raise awareness of our culture amongst the students and also try to present the culture in the most indigenous way which is both appealing to a westernised mind and also quite informative The whole idea is to de-westernize the indian minds and make them feel pride in their own culture and feel confidence in themselves and representing their culture wherever they go For this a variety of content which includes scientific representation of the scriptures so that it is convinces and provides conviction. We therefore also have launched courses and are constantly on a self improvement journey Thus by conducting cultural events like dramas and competitions students can showcase their talents and skills,this way we look to include and engage more and more students in our activities Thus putting it in short the aim is to celebrate,practice and distribute the culture of india for the experiential and evident joy of the practitioners We have just started on our journey, a long way to goâ€¦ as they say it anant se bhi aage .
